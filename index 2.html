01
InfinityX Funded
Petrol-pump experience for EVs - 30-second cell-swapping. Raised ₹9Cr, multiple IPs filed.
Hardware · EV
↗
The vision
With the goal to provide a petrol-pump like experience to Electric Vehicle users, we invented a novel method of fueling EVs that we call cell-swapping. Our proprietary technique removes all the discharged battery cells from an EV's battery pack and replaces it with fully charged cells from the station - all through a single cable.
How it works
Customer arrives at an InfinityX station and connects a single cable
Discharged cells are automatically extracted from the battery pack
Fully charged cells are swapped in from the station inventory
30 seconds later - the EV is fully charged and ready to go
Traction
Raised ₹9 Crore in funding
Filed several intellectual property patents
Proprietary cell-swapping technology - first of its kind

Hoomanly Live
A judgment-free space for genuine human connection. Built for the loneliness epidemic.
Social · Impact
↗
The mission
Millions feel isolated with nowhere safe to talk. No profiles, no pictures, no judgment. Just a presence when you need one. Privacy by design - device-based identity, client-side encryption, view-once media.
Core features
Zero-judgment anonymous chat - instant match, no sign-up
Trust Score system preventing toxicity at scale
Strangers can become Hoomans - opt-in long-term connection
AI companion for 3am when no human match is available
